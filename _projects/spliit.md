[**Spliit**](https://spliit.app) is an alternative to Splitwise. Use it to share expenses with friends and family, without creating any account.

I first built this app in 2022 while I was learning Go, then I rewrote it with Next.js and made it [open source](https://github.com/spliit-app/spliit). Many developers contribute by creating issues and pull-requests.
